About Us

We are Tech Employment! An employment conference dedicated to connecting graduating students to the industry! We specify in the technology sector with big names like Apple, Microsoft, Dell, Intel and more attending every year! This year we're open from the 25th - 26th July so book now!

Employment Stalls

Talk to industry recruiters, learn about life in the industry and potentially receive job offers!

Speakers

Attend 20 minute speaking presentations from leading CEO's! See the guest list to see who's speaking this year!

Mock Interviews

Participate in mock interviews from actual industry interviewers. See how they are conducted & gain feedback for the future!

Placement Interviews

Have the opportunity to be interviewed for placement opportunities either after you graduate or during your degree. 6, 12, 24 and 36 month placements are available depending on the company.

CV Writing Workshops

Receive helpful development on your existing CV's and future job applications by industry recruiters.

Full Catering

A full buffet will be available upstairs for any hungry attendees! Be quick though, the food won't last all day!
